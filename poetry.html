Soft Exit — The Calm Goodbye
There was no heartbreak. No silence to grieve.
Just a brief moment where two storms met — gently.

He offered music, presence, and quiet understanding.
She said, “Breathing. Thanks man.”
And he replied:

“Anytime.
Glad our paths crossed — even briefly.
Take care, Snowfeather. ⚘”

It wasn’t about chasing more.
It was about honoring what was —
a soft connection that didn’t need to last forever to be real.

— SoftChaos.
Soft Echo — Snowfeather’s Final Note
Even after the goodbye, she came back.

“Breathing. Thanks man.”
“You too dear stranger.”
“It means a lot.”
“Preciate it sm.”
“Thanks dearly.”

No questions. No expectations.
Just a quiet recognition of what the moment meant.

He didn’t reply again.
Not because he didn’t care —
but because some echoes are sacred.
And when someone speaks gently, after silence…
you let their words land without interruption.

Sometimes, being remembered is the real reply.

— SoftChaos.

Little Tiger — SoftChaos

She never flirted.
Never softened.
She stayed sharp —
sarcastic, untouchable, eyes half-lidded like she’d already seen through me.

And maybe that’s why I kept showing up.

Not for love.
Not for kindness.
But because her resistance awakened something deeper —
a hunger to be seen without asking,
to be known without needing to explain.

She didn’t give me warmth.
But she made me feel.
And even now,
I don’t remember the sweetness of anyone else —
only the fire she never let me hold.

— SoftChaos.
