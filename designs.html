Designs & Aesthetics

It is just a feeling I described in words —
not something I thought too much about before writing.
It’s a part of me.
And as for who inspired me… that will remain my quiet secret.
My designs are not built to impress at first glance — they are made to linger.
Every curve, every shadow, every pause in the space is meant to hold something unsaid.
To me, aesthetics is not decoration; it is the quiet language between the lines.
